艾米莉·狄金森《疯狂之夜》  
麦克译 
 
疯狂之夜  
愿与君享  
良辰应是  
玉宇琼浆  

风儿无奈  
心已入港  
无需海图  
无需导航  

泛舟伊甸  
爱的海洋  
我愿今夜  
泊于君旁

Wild Nights  
By Emily Dickinson

Wild Nights - Wild Nights  
Were I with thee  
Wild Nights should be  
Our luxury!

Futile - the winds -  
To the heart in port -  
Done with the compass -  
Done with the chart!

Rowing in Eden -  
Ah, the sea!  
Might I but moor Tonight -  
In Thee!
