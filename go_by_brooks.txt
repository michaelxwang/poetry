［加拿大］里奧納德·科恩《適之於溪》
麥克王翻譯

適之於溪
有魚凝睇
溪兮我愛
今我來思

適之於河
有鰻如梭
河兮我愛
凝待無多

適之於洋
有鯨巡航
洋兮我愛
我不迷茫

原文：

Go By Brooks
[Canada] Leonard Cohen (9/21/1934 - 11/7/2016)

Go by brooks, love
Where fish stare,
Go by brooks,
I will pass there.

Go by rivers,
Where eels throng,
Rivers, love,
I won't be long.

Go by oceans,
Where whales sail,
Oceans, love,
I will not fail.
