艾米莉·狄金森《疯狂之夜》
麦克王译

Wild Nights - Wild Nights
Were I with thee
与君同享夜疯狂
Wild Nights should be
Our luxury!
疯狂长夜乐无疆

Futile - the winds -
To the heart in port -
心已入港风无力
Done with the compass -
Done with the chart!
不要海图不导航

Rowing in Eden -
Ah, the sea!
泛舟伊甸爱之海
Might I but moor Tonight -
In Thee!
但愿今夜泊君旁
